About

I've spent over two decades at the intersection of live music, cultural production, and building companies. It started with collecting a bag at Istanbul Airport at 4 am for a visiting artist — and it hasn't slowed down since.


What LinkedIn doesn't quite capture: most of what I've built didn't exist before I showed up. Festivals, companies, labels, funds. Some thrived. Some were wound down with care. All of them were worth doing.


I'm based in Berlin. Through 3dots, I work as a venture builder and ecosystem connector in the creative and cultural industries — bringing together founders, resources, and financial instruments for people solving real problems in culture.

Milestones

  • 1998

    Graduated — Bursa Anatolian High School

  • 2003 – 2008

    The Istanbul Years — Freelance, Festivals & Formation

    Istanbul Events Scene · Various Roles

    It started at 4 am at Istanbul Airport, collecting a bag for a visiting artist for the Istanbul Film Festival. Over the next five years I took part in over 300 events across the city — as artist guide, production assistant, coordinator, and in whatever role needed filling.

    The range was everything Istanbul had at the time: Istanbul Film Festival, Istanbul Jazz Festival, Babylon, IF Istanbul, Akbank Jazz Festival, and music events across genres. This period was less a job and more a complete education in how live culture actually works — from the artist's perspective, the crew's, and the audience's.

    Artist Relations Production 300+ Events

  • 2008 – 2010

    Charmenko / Charm Music

    Türkiye Projects Manager & Artist Booker

    Moved from the production floor into booking — working across major and emerging acts in Türkiye and Eastern European markets. This was where freelance became professional: managing artist relationships, negotiations, and touring projects at regional scale.

    Artist Booking Türkiye & Eastern Europe

  • 2009

    Left Istanbul University

    Business Management Program — after 6 years

    Six years is a long time to stay somewhere you're not sure about. Eventually left without completing the degree. The real education had been happening in parallel — backstage, in production offices, and on tour.

  • 2010 – 2014

    Pozitif Live

    Projects & Production · Shareholder (for a period)

    Four years at one of Türkiye's defining live entertainment companies — joining the projects and production department and becoming a shareholder for a period. The work covered the full range of what Turkish live music looked like at its peak.

    Projects included Rock'n Coke, Cirque du Soleil's Saltimbanco and Alegria Turkish runs, Efes Pilsen One Love Festival, Efes Pilsen Blues Festival, Akbank Jazz Festival, and major one-off concerts — including U2's Istanbul show and Red Hot Chili Peppers' Istanbul show.

    Rock'n Coke Cirque du Soleil U2 Istanbul RHCP Istanbul Akbank Jazz

  • ~2012 – 2014

    Graduated — Istanbul Bilgi University

    Management of Performing Arts

    The degree that actually fit. After years in the industry and six years at Istanbul University's Business Management program, finding the right academic frame — late, but alongside real-world practice — mattered.

  • 2015

    Moved to Berlin

    A deliberate shift. Berlin in 2015 was one of the world's most important cities for music, independent culture, and the kind of creative economy I wanted to build in.

  • 2018 – 2021

    XJAZZ Berlin

    Co-Founder & Co-CEO · Still a Shareholder

    Co-founded and co-ran XJAZZ out of Berlin, building it into an international festival format. Editions ran in Türkiye, Belarus, and Sweden (the latter cancelled due to COVID), alongside the main event in Germany.

    I remain a shareholder — with full support and love for what it stands for.

    Festival Jazz Berlin International

  • Since 2018

    3dots

    Co-Founder · Venture Builder · Ecosystem Builder

    3dots is the long-running vehicle through which I've co-founded and supported multiple projects at the intersection of culture, music, and entrepreneurship. What started as a project company has evolved into an ecosystem builder — connecting founders, resources, and financial instruments across the creative and cultural industries.

    Projects launched or co-founded through 3dots:

    Bozcaada Jazz Festival — reaching its 10th edition in 2026.

    XJAZZ Istanbul, Ankara, İzmir — regional expansions of the XJAZZ concept.

    Bergama Theatre Festival — exited in 2025, with deep pride in what it became. Still a fan and a curator of the Akropol events in its closing chapter.

    Space Goats (label) — launched and wound down. Couldn't find its footing.

    Lycia Records — ongoing.

    Kabak & Lin Records — formed from a merger with Lin Records; exited.

    Erişilebilir Her Şey — an accessibility consultancy co-founded to help cultural operators adopt accessibility principles. Exited after formation due to structural changes. The idea was right.

    3dots currently runs accelerators and funds, acts as a venture builder, and raises financial instruments for creative and cultural industry founders.

    Bozcaada Jazz XJAZZ Istanbul Accelerator Venture Building

  • 2021 – 2024

    Deplike

    Co-Founder · Music Tech · London & Ankara

    My first real step into tech — something I'd wanted for a long time. Deplike is a music technology company. I came in as co-founder, raised seed investment, and oversaw global partnerships. Left in 2024 to focus on what was building at 3dots.

    Crossing from cultural production into a tech startup was a significant shift.

    Music Tech Seed Stage Global Partnerships
